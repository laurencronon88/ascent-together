Our Speakers

Two leaders. Decades of experience transforming brands, teams, and industries.

01
Keynote Speaker
Greg
Creed
Chairman Emeritus, Collider
Retired CEO, Yum! Brands
Led Yum! Brands for 25 years — the global home of Taco Bell, KFC, and Pizza Hut. Drove the "Think Outside the Bun" campaign, five consecutive years of growth, and the global expansion of the Taco Bell brand.

Greg Creed was Chief Executive Officer of Yum! Brands from January 2015 to December 2019 and served as a Director of the Board through May 2020. He was appointed CEO of Taco Bell in 2011 after serving as President and Chief Concept Officer, and previously served as CMO and COO for Yum! Brands.

Creed earned a business degree from Queensland University of Technology in Brisbane, Australia, was named 2014 QUT Alumnus of the Year, and was awarded an honorary doctorate in 2019. He serves on the Boards of Whirlpool Corporation (presiding director), Aramark Corporation, and Delta Air Lines. Since retiring, he founded Creed UnCo — a focused advisory practice on brand building, franchising, culture, and leadership.
02
Industry Expert
Patrick
Noone
President, Technomic
Food Strategy & Marketing
Seasoned food industry executive with deep expertise in competitive analysis, consumer insights, and strategic planning. Leads Technomic's research and advisory practice across the full food service landscape.

Patrick Noone currently serves as President at Technomic, where he has held a rich succession of leadership roles including Senior Vice President and Executive Vice President. He specializes in competitive analysis, customer insights, and strategic planning across the food service industry.

Patrick leads cross-functional teams and drives market research initiatives that inform business strategy and enhance customer engagement for some of the industry's most recognizable brands. He is a recognized voice on sustainable practices and evolving consumer preferences in the food service sector.
